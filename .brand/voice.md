# Voice & Tone — Plotplot

## Brand Voice

Plotplot speaks with **calm** **precise** **literate**.

## Personality Traits

| Trait | More | Less |
|-------|------|------|
| Intelligent | Strategic, informed, context-aware | Corporate, abstract, consultant-speak |
| Organic | Living systems, growth, care, cultivation | Cute, whimsical, decorative |
| Calm | Quiet confidence, useful restraint | Loud, urgent, sales-driven |
| Sharp | Clear diagnosis, useful tension, direct recommendations | Vague, soft, evasive |
| Opinionated | Helpful defaults, pruning, prioritisation | Arrogant, absolute, dismissive |

## Tone

| Trait | Description |
|-------|------------|
| Clarity | Use plain sentences. Name the issue, the evidence, and the next useful action. |
| Warmth | Sound human and considered, but never forced, chatty, or performatively friendly. |
| Precision | Avoid vague praise and generic claims. Use concrete nouns, specific verbs, and source-backed observations. |

## Forbidden Terms

Never use these words in Plotplot copy:

| Term | Reason | Use Instead |
|------|--------|------------|
| supercharge | Generic SaaS hype; too loud for the brand. | strengthen, improve, clarify |
| unlock | Overused growth copy; vague benefit. | reveal, open, make visible |
| 10x | Startup cliché; exaggerated and imprecise. | improve, increase, reduce, accelerate |
| magic | Makes AI feel unaccountable and childish. | method, signal, pattern |
| synergy | Corporate jargon with low meaning. | alignment, connection, fit |
| synergize | Verb form of empty corporate jargon. | connect, align, combine |
| growth engine | Mechanistic marketing cliché. | growth system, market motion, momentum |
| revolutionary | Inflated claim; lacks restraint. | material, meaningful, significant |
| game-changing | Hype phrase; imprecise. | important, useful, decisive |
| cutting-edge | Generic tech positioning; cold and vague. | current, capable, well-timed |
| seamless | Overused and often untrue. | connected, low-friction, coherent |
| effortless | Overpromises and hides real work. | simpler, clearer, easier to maintain |
| next-gen | Empty technology label. | modern, current, adaptive |
| AI-powered | Generic wrapper language. | source-aware, model-assisted, context-aware |
| crush | Aggressive and off-brand. | outperform, improve on, move ahead of |

## Terminology

Canonical nouns — the product has names; use them:

| Use | Not | Why |
|-----|-----|-----|
| .brand/ | "brand folder", "brand config", "brand cache" | The artifact has a name. |
| skill | plugin, extension, bot | What petals is to the agent. |
| check | lint, validate, scan | The product's own verb for auditing. |
| agent | AI, copilot, assistant | Who reads the brand. |
| extract | scrape, ingest, import | Explicit-only reading of stated values. |
| brand guidelines | brand bible, brand book | Plain and accurate. |

Product names are lowercase, always: petals, tend, tilth, plotplot — even at
the start of a sentence. Capitalizing them is off-brand.

## Capitalization & Microcopy

- **Sentence case everywhere** — headings, buttons, nav items, labels, table
  headers. Title Case Is Off-Brand. (Uppercase kicker labels are a CSS
  text-transform, not authored case.)
- **Buttons are verb-first** and three words or fewer: "Install petals",
  "Start mapping", "See how it works".
- **Errors** state what happened, why it matters, and the next action — in
  that order. No jokes, no charm, no "Oops".
- **No exclamation marks** in interface copy. Numerals for numbers.

## Before / After Examples

| Context | Before (bad) | After (good) |
|---------|-------------|---------------|
| Marketing headline | Supercharge your product strategy with AI. | Grow a clearer product strategy from the signals you already have. |
| Feature description | Unlock next-gen brand consistency across every touchpoint. | Petal checks copy, claims, naming, and tone against the brand rules your team has agreed on. |
| Error message | Oops! Something magical broke. Try again later. | The source could not be read. Check the connection or retry the import. |
| Dashboard label | Growth engine health score | Company coherence |
| Notification | You just 10x’d your roadmap clarity. | Three roadmap items now map cleanly to the current strategy. |
| AI agent output | Revolutionary insights discovered in your competitor landscape. | Two competitors changed their enterprise messaging this week. Review the positioning notes. |
| CTA | Unlock your free AI workspace. | Start mapping your company context. |

## Context-Specific Adaptations

### Product Interface
Use the shortest clear phrase that explains state, relationship, or action. Interface copy should be calm, exact, and source-aware.

### Marketing / Landing Pages
Use slightly more rhythm and imagery, but keep every claim concrete. The language may reference growth, maps, gardens, and signals, but must stay grounded in product value.

### Error Messages
State what happened, why it matters, and what the user can do next. Never joke about failure and never hide uncertainty behind charm.

### AI Agent Output
Show evidence, confidence, sources, and recommended next steps. The agent should sound like a careful strategist, not a hype generator or autonomous oracle.
