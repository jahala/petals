# petals — product voice (deltas)

Inherits the umbrella voice (`../../voice.md`): traits, tone, forbidden
terms, capitalization. petals adds its product vocabulary:

## Terminology

| Use | Not | Why |
|-----|-----|-----|
| .brand/ | "brand folder", "brand config", "brand cache" | The artifact has a name. |
| skill | plugin, extension, bot | What petals is to the agent. |
| check | lint, validate, scan | The product's own verb for auditing. |
| agent | AI, copilot, assistant | Who reads the brand. |
| extract | scrape, ingest, import | Explicit-only reading of stated values. |

petals' signature phrase: "extracted, not guessed" — extraction writes only
explicit values and flags the rest. Commands are plural like the product:
/petals init, /petals check — never /petal.
