# petals — product colors (deltas)

The palette is the umbrella's (`../../colors.md`). petals adds no colors — it
claims its accent and a usage rule:

| Role | Hex | Usage |
|------|-----|-------|
| Product accent | #E8917F | petals' own marks, pins, washes — the petal of the fan |

Other products' accents (tend #D97757, stem #4E6A4A, …) are product markers,
not petals UI colors — `/petals check` warns when one appears in petals
output.
